Our Curriculum

Our strong belief at this school is that we should "Shine with light In All We Do" and that we can show it in our talents attitude and efforts.Our knowledge-based value-led Curriculum aims to provide all our pupils with the transferable knowledge throughout the stages in their lives.This knowledge needs to be fun recallable and fixed in their minds.Our school want your children to live their life to the fullest but also live successful ones. We also want to teach your child to contribute positively to society and recognize learning as a life long gift that will open many great opportunities. We are proud of our Curriculum at West Millers Primary School and consider it to be the heart of our school.The teachers at our school plan their lessons carefully and here are some examples of what they do: . providing suitable learning challenges which build on prior knowledge; . increasingly developing the children's fluency and mastery of key skills; . responding to pupils individual and diverse needs; . overcoming any potential barriers to learning We hope that your child will fit into our school and shine the brightest they have ever shined!

Learning Hub

Our learning Hub is our way of giving key information to children's parents.The learning hub tells you what topic your child will be focusing on each term and what book their class will be focusing on.There are also many things your child can do at home to help with their learning including:EdShed TTRockstars and Boom Reader.Our learning hub also tells parents about events and awards like shining star of the week or role model of the week.Our school love to find ways to encourage children to lean towards good behavior and welcoming communities.We have a learning hub from EYFS (Early Years Foundation Stages ) to Year 6.Also to keep you and your children safe we have 4 members of staff which we call our Safeguarding Team the 4 members of our Safeguarding Team are Ms Sakiru Head teacher of West Millers Primary School, Mr Smith Deputy Head of West Millers Primary School, Mrs Thorn Caretaker of West Millers and Mrs Fudge Head of Early Years Foundation Stages.

about us

SkriptaPod ICT Hub Limited is a training institute and software development company founded in January, 2020 in the city of Ibadan. Our competitive edge alongside new training techniques puts us at the forefront of training services. We are living in an age where computer knowledge is a necessity and the demand for ICT skills is on an upward trajectory. Our training center differs from the traditional ICT training services (usually offered by colleges and universities) because of our robust teaching-learning process which caters to individual desires.

SkriptaPod provides individuals, medium-to-large scale companies, and government (agencies and institutes) with workshop and training programs in areas such as computer software development, computer systems, and database training, graphics design and animation, management training, and career development.

At Skriptapod, we are passionate about the pursuit of excellence with uncompromising standards and integrity. We are in the industry to make a positive impact and lasting impression. We are certain that our core values and the quality of service we offer will assist our trainees in driving them to enviable heights. We are in the IT training Industry to favorably compete with other leading brands in the industry both in Nigeria and across the world. As a company, we are willing to go the extra mile to invest in some of the finest professionals ( ICT trainers/instructors/facilitators) also, we have put processes and structures in place that will ensure that we are always at the top of our game when it comes to impacting knowledge as it relates to our ICT training services.

Our Mission

To understand what our clientele hope to achieve and dedicate our ICT training and development to the fulfillment of those goals thus, creating a tactile experience.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become an industry leader and the standard for competence and excellence in ICT training.
